Why Hayat Care

A different kind of care company

We started Hayat Care because we couldn't find a home care provider that truly honoured our own families' values. So we built one.

Every carer is employed directly (never zero-hours), fully trained, DBS-checked, and matched to clients with care. We pay above the National Living Wage because we believe good care starts with being cared for ourselves.

How it works

Four simple steps to the right care

From first call to first visit, we make the process calm and uncomplicated. Most families have a care plan in place within a week.

  1. 1

    Free care consultation

    A 20-minute phone call with our care manager. We listen, answer questions, and explain how we can help — no obligation, no pressure.

  2. 2

    In-home assessment

    We visit your home (or hospital discharge lounge) to understand needs, routines, preferences and culture. A family member is welcome to be there.

  3. 3

    Personalised care plan

    You receive a clear written plan covering visits, tasks, costs and goals. We adjust until it's exactly right.

  4. 4

    Meet your carer

    Introduced to a small, consistent team matched to your loved one. Same faces, familiar routine, real relationship.
